How It Works

Precision without contact

A seamless system that bridges physical and digital — nothing but a webcam and two green rings.

Camera Calibration

Position your webcam facing the screen. TouchlessTouch initializes an intelligent visual field and prepares the tracking pipeline in seconds.

4-Point Screen Mapping

Tap each corner of your display once. The system builds a precise spatial grid mapping every pixel into the camera's perspective.

Cursor Control Ring

Wear a small green ring on your right index finger. Its position inside the calibrated zone translates directly into smooth cursor movement.

Smart Click Detection

A green ring on the left hand acts as click trigger. Move it outside the active zone to click or hold it for drag operations — natural and instant.
