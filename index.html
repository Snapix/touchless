HOW IT WORKS

Your camera.
Your fingers.
Your control.

01

Position Your Camera

Place your webcam in front of your laptop or monitor screen and launch the TouchlessTouch engine.

02

Calibrate the Screen

Click the four corners of your monitor as seen through the camera feed — this maps your physical screen into the tracking space.

03

Wear Your Green Rings

Slip on DIY green rings on both index fingers. Right finger moves the cursor. Left finger triggers clicks.

04

Gesture & Go

When the left green finger leaves the calibrated area and returns, it registers as a click or move — based on how long the gesture is held.

Touchless Cursor Control

Move your mouse pointer using only hand gestures captured through a standard webcam. No special hardware required.

Smooth & Responsive Tracking

Real-time color-segmentation tracking that stays accurate and fluid — even under variable lighting conditions.

Lightweight & Fast

Zero background bloat. Starts instantly, runs lean, and lets your other apps breathe — built for everyday use.

Designed for Windows 10 & 11

Native Windows experience. Tight OS integration with Fluent-style UI and full compatibility across modern Windows builds.

Built with Accessibility in Mind

Designed for users who benefit from hands-free interaction — motor impairments, limited mobility, or just a desire for a next-gen interface experience.
