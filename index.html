How It Works

Precision without contact

A seamless system that bridges the physical and digital with nothing but a camera.

Camera Calibration

Position your webcam facing the screen. TouchlessTouch initializes an intelligent visual field and prepares the tracking pipeline in seconds.

4-Point Screen Mapping

Tap each corner of your display once. The system creates a precise spatial grid — mapping every pixel of your screen into the camera's perspective.

Cursor Control

Wear a small green ring on your right-hand index finger. Its position within the calibrated zone translates directly into smooth, lag-free cursor movement.

Smart Click Detection

A green ring on the left hand acts as the click trigger. Extend it outside the active zone to fire a click or hold it for drag operations — naturally and intuitively.
